## â“ Common Questions

**Q: How do I start outreach for multiple leads?**
A: Go to Outreach page, check the boxes next to leads, then click "Start Outreach" button.

**Q: Why can't I start outreach?**
A: The lead must have an email address. Add an email in the Leads page first.

**Q: Where do I see responses?**
A: Go to the Follow-ups page. All leads who responded will be listed there.

**Q: How do I reply to a lead?**
A: Go to Follow-ups page, find the lead, click "Reply" button, type your message, and send.

**Q: My emails aren't sending automatically.**
A: Automation is still in development (Milestone 4). Manual workflows are recommended for now.
